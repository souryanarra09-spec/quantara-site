What are QuickBooks and Xero?

They're the accounting software most small businesses already use to track their money — every sale, every bill, every payment. The software organizes it all into the standard financial statements automatically.

Quantara doesn't replace them. It sits on top. Your financial data already lives in QuickBooks or Xero, neatly organized — the problem is it's hard to understand. Quantara is the translator that turns it into something you can actually use.
